About the Alumni Cup

The Alumni Cup is an annual athletic competition bringing together university alumni from across the nation. Founded in 2025 by the University of Texas at Austin and the University of Michigan, we celebrate school spirit, healthy competition, and lifelong connections through golf and 10K running.
