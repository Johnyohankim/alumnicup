About the Alumni Cup

ì•Œë£¸ë‚˜ì´ ì»µ ì†Œê°œ

The Alumni Cup is an annual athletic competition that brings together university alumni from across the nation. Founded in 2025 by the University of Texas at Austin and the University of Michigan at Ann Arbor, it celebrates school spirit, healthy competition, and lifelong connections through golf and 10K running. Each year, the winning school has its name engraved on the trophy and retains it for one year. The trophy features seven engraving slots on each of its four sides, allowing 28 years of champions to be permanently commemorated.
